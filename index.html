About Us

Introduction

CM Recycling and Reuse Pty Ltd IS dedicated to advancing sustainable Land Care practices, particularly agroecology, in rural, peri-urban, and Rural communities. Established in 2025 by Mthetho Mkhungo, our company is committed to improving the livelihoods of communities through Environmental impact and Circular Economy by means of Recycling and Reuse.

At CM Recycling and Reuse Pty Ltd, we’re committed to driving positive environmental impact and enhancing livelihoods in rural and peri-urban communities. Through innovative agroecology practices and a circular economy approach, we are shaping a sustainable future for all.

Our Focus Areas

Our Focus Areas

1. Environmental Promotion

We actively promote ecological practices, emphasizing sustainable land management, recycling, and reuse of products.

2. Community Education

We provide training and workshops to equip communities with the skills and knowledge needed to adopt agroecological practices.

3. Research and Development

We invest in research to develop and disseminate best practices in ecology, aiming to continually improve land use techniques.

4. Partnerships and Collaboration

We collaborate with local and international organizations, government agencies, and stakeholders to leverage resources and amplify our impact.

Future Goals

Our future goal is to expand our work in rural and peri-urban communities in all the nine province of South Africa we are passionate about improving the live-hood of our people in the marginalized communities.

As we reach more people it is important that we develop our own ecological hub where we will showcase sustainable practise of recycling. We will develop ecological model whereby people can enhance skills, capacity and eco-friendly systems. We would like to partner with likeminded organizations and agencies to steer the ship in increasing the awareness of agro-ecology and permaculture systems
